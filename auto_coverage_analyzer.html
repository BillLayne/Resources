North Carolina Requirements

Legal Minimum Requirements

NC law requires minimum coverage, but it may not fully protect your assets.

Required Coverage

  • Bodily Injury (per person) $30,000
  • Bodily Injury (per accident) $60,000
  • Property Damage $25,000
  • Estimated Monthly Cost $45 - $75

Coverage Gaps

  • Low limits may not cover serious accidents
  • No coverage for your own vehicle damage
  • No protection against uninsured drivers
  • Personal assets at risk in lawsuits
